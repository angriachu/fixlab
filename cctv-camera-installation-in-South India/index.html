What are the procedures in a security camera installation in South India?

The steps in installing a security camera and DVR include mounting the camera, placing the DVR, managing the cables, connecting the wires, and powering the system.

No, you can get video surveillance anytime, anywhere without an internet or WiFi connection.

What are the types of CCTV available in the market?

Security cameras are available in monochrome or color. Bullet CCTV cameras, Board CCTV cameras, vandal-proof CCTV cameras, IP network CCTV cameras, high-speed dome CCTV cameras, PTC cameras, Infrared/Night Vision CCTV cameras and Zoom cameras are the different options available in the market.

Which is best? IP/Analog CCTV camera or HD-TVI CCTV camera?

The best technology in CCTV systems is the IP cameras which use compression and digital encoding for capturing videos at good resolutions.
